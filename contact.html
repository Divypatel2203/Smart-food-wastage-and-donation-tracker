About Vadodara

Vadodara, formerly known as Baroda, is the third-largest city in the Gujarat State of India. It serves as the administrative headquarters of the Vadodara District and is located on the banks of the Vishwamitri River.

Known for its rich cultural heritage, educational institutions, and industrial growth, Vadodara is an ideal location for our NoWaste initiative to make a significant impact in reducing food waste and helping communities.
