Guidelines for Donating

  • Ensure all food items are unexpired and in good condition

  • Preferably donate non-perishable items such as canned goods, pasta, rice, and cereals

  • For perishable items, proper storage and temperature control is essential

  • Food must be in original packaging with ingredient lists when possible

  • Prepare food items for transportation by placing in secure, clean containers
