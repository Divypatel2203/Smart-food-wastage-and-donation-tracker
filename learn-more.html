Our Mission

Food wastage is a critical issue globally, while millions of people struggle with hunger daily. Smart Food Wastage & Donation Tracker is a web-based platform designed to minimize food wastage by connecting restaurants, hotels, and households with NGOs and needy individuals. This system enables users to list surplus food, which can be efficiently tracked and redistributed to those in need.

The platform ensures seamless donation management through real-time tracking, smart logistics, and a transparent donation system. By leveraging technology, this project promotes sustainability, reduces food waste, and helps bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger. With an intuitive interface and automated notifications, it encourages responsible food redistribution and enhances community welfare.

Our mission is to minimize food waste and maximize food security by empowering individuals and organizations to make informed decisions about their food consumption and donation habits. We believe that by working together, we can create a world where no one goes hungry and where food resources are used responsibly.

The Impact

Environmental Impact

Food waste in landfills produces methane, a potent greenhouse gas. By reducing food waste, we can significantly decrease greenhouse gas emissions and conserve the resources used in food production.

Social Impact

While millions of tons of food are wasted, many people go hungry. Our platform bridges this gap by channeling surplus food to those who need it most, creating stronger communities.

Economic Impact

Food waste costs households and businesses billions annually. Our system helps users track and reduce waste, saving money while supporting local economies.
