About Vadodara

Vadodara (formerly known as Baroda) is the third-largest city in Gujarat, India. Known as the Cultural Capital of Gujarat, it is home to the historic Laxmi Vilas Palace and Maharaja Sayajirao University.

Our food donation network in Vadodara has established collection points at key locations throughout the city, making it easier for donors to contribute to reducing hunger and food waste.
